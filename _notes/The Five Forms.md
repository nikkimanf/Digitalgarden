These steps can be achieved through the application of five “forms” or template. By following the steps in these forms, individuals are able to assess their abilities, select long-term goals and achieve self-reliance. That individual success can be leveraged into professional success and support a Lean environment, particularly by focusing on the [[Eight types of waste | eighth waste]] – skills and talent.

The templates range from simple to detailed. The most important note to remember is that individuals must fill out these forms (i.e., do the work) themselves. (A mentor or coach can help guide the individual.)
